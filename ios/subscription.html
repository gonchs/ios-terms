Subscription Terms

1. The subscription will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24-hours before the end of the current period.

2. The subscription may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase.

3. Any unused portion of a free trial period, if offered, will be forfeited when the user purchases a subscription to that publication, where applicable.

4. Payment will be charged to the user's iTunes Account at confirmation of purchase.

5. Subscriptions may include additional features or content that may be unlocked during the subscription period.

6. Prices for subscriptions may vary depending on the subscription package and the length of the subscription.

7. The user's subscription will automatically renew at the end of the subscription period unless auto-renew is turned off.

8. The user may cancel their subscription at any time, but this will not take effect until the end of the current subscription period.
